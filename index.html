ΔHEnthalpy Change
ΔH = H(products) − H(reactants). Negative means exothermic; positive means endothermic. Does not determine reaction speed.
⚡Activation Energy
Minimum energy molecules need to react. Ea(rev) = Ea(fwd) − ΔH = kJ/mol. Higher T → more molecules exceed Ea → faster rate.
‡Transition State
Highest-energy point on the pathway. Bonds are partially broken and formed. Cannot be isolated — exists only momentarily.
